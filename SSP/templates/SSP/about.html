About The System

The School Services Portal (SSP) is a web-based application developed to simplify document requests and student services. It allows students to access important school documents, submit requests online, and track their applications conveniently from anywhere.

Our Mission

To provide students with a faster, more accessible, and user-friendly way of requesting academic documents while improving administrative efficiency.
